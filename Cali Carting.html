Residential and Commercial Dumpster Rentals in New Jersey

Cali Carting Inc. is a full-service solid waste and recycling company conveniently located in Kearny, NJ. Since 1983, we have provided our customers throughout New Jersey with dependable commercial, industrial, and residential waste and recycling services. We have been New Jersey’s top residential and commercial dumpster rental company for over 35 years, offering guaranteed next day delivery, convenient delivery options, and friendly customer service. Let “The Man With The Can” help you or your business with your waste and recycling needs.

Our Dumpster Rental Sizes

Cali Carting offers several dumpster rental sizes, ranging from 10 to 30 yards, for all types of projects. Our dumpsters are used for a variety of waste types, including construction debris, demolition, household waste, furniture, and more. Whether you need a dumpster for a residential cleanout project or are looking to optimize your business’ waste disposal process with a commercial dumpster rental, we’ll help you choose the container that best suits your needs.
