Welcome to OrthoGeorgia

At OrthoGeorgia, our mission is to provide a comprehensive team approach with time-honored commitment to compassionate care for an excellent outcome.

The strong foundation established over the past sixty years positions OrthoGeorgia to continue to meet the mission of excellence and provide first rate, comprehensive orthopaedic care for our patients. We are proud to serve the communities throughout Central Georgia and give those in the area access to exceptional orthopaedic care and services tailored to them.

At OrthoGeorgia, there is nothing more important than the health and well-being of our patients, employees and community. OrthoGeorgia is following the CDC recommended best practices to provide you and our employees with a safe environment:
